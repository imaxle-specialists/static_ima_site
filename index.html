Who we are

IMA Group is an end-to-end product partner to the global auto parts industry.

Through leading market insight, unparalleled technical product knowledge and complete control of our vertical supply chain we listen to, predict and respond to the evolving needs of our customers.

Our innovative approach enables us, and our customers, to be the first to market with new and unique products – often fulfilling untapped consumer demand before it arises.

With global scale, but the ability to adapt to the local requirements of every market we serve, we work in partnership with our customers to deliver the automotive parts they need in a way that makes sense to them – ensuring every project runs smoothly by adapting our operations to align with their own.

Our strategically located operations centres uniquely serve the needs of local markets – providing the parts our customers need at the right time, in the right way and for the right prices.
